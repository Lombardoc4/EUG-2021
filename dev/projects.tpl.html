Our garden work is all the buzz

Evolution Urban Grows is dedicated to moving and incorporating native plants into mainstream landscaping practices. Our aim is to encourage the use of native plants and eliminate the use of invasive species in public landscapes.

We help you choose plants that are better for the environment, better for pollinators and better for your well-being. We provide expertly selected native plants that will bring you peace of mind, help you reconnect with nature and invite life to your garden.
