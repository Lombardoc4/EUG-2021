Native plants create a real buzz

Evolution Urban Grows (EUG) is commited to restoring native plants in urban landscapes. We believe it is vital to re‑introduce the organic beauty of these plants to gardens of all sizes. Natives cause a rippling effect of excitement and create opportunity for education.

From plant and environmental consulting, to garden design and installation, we provide a wide range of services. Strategic and diligent work creates long lasting connections and continued support.

Why EUG?

We bring the buzz to your garden

EUG assists you in choose plants that are better for the environment, better for pollinators and better for your well‑being. Expertly selected native plants will bring you peace of mind and help you reconnect with nature. Our plantings, in their natural habitats, bring success and life to your garden.
